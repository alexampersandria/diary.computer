## Definitions

- Maintainers: Any individual who has contributed to the development and maintenance of diary.computer.
- diary.computer: The open source platform developed by the maintainers, all code is available on the repository [alexampersandria/diary.computer](https://github.com/alexampersandria/diary.computer)
- Application: Refers to diary.computer and any associated services provided by the maintainers which uses the diary.computer platform. This is not limited to the diary.computer website itself, but also includes any future applications or services built using the diary.computer platform/api.

## Information We Collect

Without an account, we do not collect any personal information.

When you create an account, we collect the following personal information (given at sign up):

- Email address
- Display Name (may be your personal name or any other name you choose

When you sign up you agree that you are legally allowed to provide this information and that it is accurate. And that you are legally allowed to enter into binding contracts and use this application. As such we do not collect information about your age or birthdate, but instead require users to be of legal age in their respective country to use this application.

When using the application, we may collect certain technical information automatically, this is stored securely and not shared with any third parties. This includes:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited (only kept in logs, not stored permanently)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. This includes:

- Creating and managing your account
- Communicating with you about your account and our services
- Analyzing usage patterns to improve our application and services

## Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with third-party services for their own marketing purposes. We may use third-party services to help us operate our application, such as hosting providers or database services. All servers are hosted within the European Union. It is also possible for you to self host the application using the open source code available on GitHub if you do not wish to use our hosted services.

The application is hosted on, or using services from:

- DigitalOcean: Used for hosting and infrastructure services. Refer to their [privacy policy](https://www.digitalocean.com/legal/privacy-policy/) for more information. DigitalOceans app platform uses cloudflare as a CDN, for more information see their [privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).
- PlanetScale: Used for database services.
- GitHub: Used for source code hosting and version control. Refer to their [privacy policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) for more information.

Any third-party services we use are carefully selected to ensure they comply with data protection regulations. We do not share your personal information with these third-party services for their own marketing purposes. All services used for running the application are hosted within the European Union.
